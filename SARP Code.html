Who We Are

San Andreas Roleplay

Since 2010, we have dedicated ourselves to our members. We are a family of friends, coming together to take on the challenge of roleplaying as first responders in a plethora of games (primarily GTA V). With enhanced vehicles, scripting, and common sense policy, we're here to have fun, patrol, make some arrests, and occasionally just mess around in our great FiveM server!

Why us?

We put our members first! While we have a chain-of-command and maintain a set of policies, we stand strong to our founding tenant - we are real people, here to be friends and have fun together! Our rank structure only applies in-game. Outside of game, we basically sit around, hang out, and "shoot the shit!" We are a group built by our members, for our members.

The Los Santos County Police

In today's growing climate, the LSCP is here, working tirelessly to enhance the quality of life for all those within the Los Santos and Blaine Counties. We hold ourselves to the highest standard to make sure Los Santos can count on us to be there whenever they need us! Enforce the law, fight crime, and earn that free Bean Machine coffee!
